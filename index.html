How does bonuses work?

There are two bonus types, “Bonus Money” and “Free Spins”. They are similar, but Free Spins are played on specific games.

How Bonus Money works.

  • Bonus Money is a sum you’re given to play with.
  • You can’t withdraw a bonus balance until you fulfilled the wagering conditions, meaning you have to play through the bonus balance. The standard playthrough condition is 35 times your received bonus sum.
  • Once the bonus has been played through, the bonus balance will turn into real money. 
  • When we offer bonuses, it’s regularly offered on your next deposit. For example, a deposit offer of 50% up to €100 will give you 50% of your deposit as a bonus up to a maximum amount of €100.

How Free Spins works.

  • Imagine Free Spins as a “free ride” on a game and something that determines how much bonus you will receive.
  • Free Spins are offered to you on a specific game, and they’re also provided with a specific amount of spins (rounds) and a value per spin.
  • The outcome of your Free Spins regarding how much you’ve won will be turned into Bonus Money once you finish your last Free Spin.

What documents are needed to verify my account?

The documents required for verifying your account are outlined below.

Documents for identity verification

  • Accepted documents - A picture of your passport, ID card or driver’s licence.
  • Document requirements - The document must be valid (not expired) and display the expiration date.

Documents for verifying your proof of address

  • Accepted documents - PDF or photo of a utility bill, bank or credit card statement or similar official letters.
  • Document requirements - The document can’t be over 3 months old and must contain your full name, address, date of issue, and a logo or clear representation of the sender. The entire document must be visible (all four corners).

Documents for verifying your payment methods (depending on the method(s) used)

  • Accepted documents for credit/debit cards - A picture of your credit card showing the first 6 and last 4 digits, your name and expiration date.
  • Document requirements - Details between the first 6 and last 4 digits can be covered. If you have a physical card, the card must be signed. Please remember that cards are designed differently and that you might need to take a picture of both sides of the card to fulfil the requirements.
  • Accepted documents for banking - A picture of your physical bank statement or a PDF of your online bank statement.
  • Document requirements - The information on the statement must contain your name, date of issue, account info, bank logo, and at least one transaction. Online screenshots are accepted if they show all the necessary information in one picture and the Page URL.
  • Accepted documents for e-wallets - A screenshot of your e-wallet account with registered details such as name, email and/or phone number. If applicable, your address, date of birth and User/Account ID.
